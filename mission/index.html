Mission Statement | June 6th 2026

The Satoshi Times: Genesis

The Signal is illusive, but if you pay attention, you can find it in the noise. The Satoshi Times aims to be that signal in the noise. A record of Bitcoin’s ideas, history, future, place in time and mysteries.

The Satoshi Times exists to treat Bitcoin history with the seriousness it deserves in the public record. The Satoshi Times is for readers who want primary source-first reporting and analysis.

Our masthead motto is Veritas Aequitas LiberƎtas: Truth, Justice, and Liberty Exist. Veritas means we test claims and trace them to primary sources wherever possible. Aequitas means the record will be handled with balance. LiberƎtas points to the deeper promise associated with Bitcoin.

The Satoshi Times will publish as part newspaper, part research index, part cultural gazette. Each article is meticulous and should leave the reader with a clearer map of what happened, and where we are going. Let us proceed together apace. Onward.
